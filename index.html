90 mins, no intermission. Gravity-defying acrobatics, martial arts, pyrotechnics on a 360° rotating stage that goes vertical and disappears into the floor. Nothing else like it.

Rotating lineup of top-tier stand-up comics. Same building as KÀ — more casual, lower stakes, reliably hilarious. Great fallback if anyone in the group isn't a Cirque person.
